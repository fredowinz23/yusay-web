LIVELIHOOD PROGRAM

The Felix G. Yusay Foundation's Livelihood program is designed to empower individuals and communities by providing a platform for sustainable economic growth

The foundation's focus in this initiative is on enhancing the lives of the economically disadvantaged. The goal of this program is to help people and places become economically self-sufficient and raise their standard of living. 

People can participate in the program to improve their skill sets. They've also helped them out by donating some resources that are renewable.
