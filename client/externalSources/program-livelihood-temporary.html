Our Mission


To serve and enhance the quality of life of the poor and the needy through giving them access to education, medical assistance, financial support, and sport engagement that enables them to achieve their full potential, at the same time to promote environmental protection and preservation and livelihood for self-sufficiency in Negros Occidental.

OUR VISION

Better quality of life the of the poorest of the poor in Negros Occiendental

Educational Program

Education builds a better future and better communities. FGYFI Education Program is dedicated to giving opportunities and access to better education to poor but deserving students from indigent families.

Health Program

The FGYFI Health Program helps for the improvement of nutrition and health condition of poor families.

Environmental Program

Environmental Program is driven to promote environmental sustainability through encouraging, inspiring, informing, and enabling people to care and support for the protection, preservation, and conservation of the environment and natural resources.

Livelihood Program

Provide income opportunities for poor families to improve their quality of life.

Sports Program

Encourage underprivileged young children and youth to get involved in sports-related activities to improve their physical capabilities.

Special Program

Special Program is an immediate assistance provided to victims of calamities or disasters, etc.
